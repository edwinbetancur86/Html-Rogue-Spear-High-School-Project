Terrorist Hunt



Team Terrorist Elemination


This Operation includes big teams in code name such as alpha, bravo, and charle.
This operation is necessary if there is a massive amount of damage and corruption with a
commuity or sometimes a country, uselly there's a negitive plot formed by large armed forces
for large plantions. If such actions are taken, a terrorist hunt operation is made to eleminate
a large amount of targets. In this option, you can call out teams in a group of four, each team has
up to a maxinmum of four special force soldiers, the teams are color coded along with their group
code name. In some missions, you will need a sniper to do his job while you send other individuals
to do their seperated plans, you can assign their plans. Weapons needed for this operation are uselly ones
that are loud and cause the most damage, for an all out weapon, the SWAT and the COLT COMMANDO are good
choices. You will also be needing a side weapon such as a heart beat sensor or jammer(that works best).
Go to www.3dretreat.com if you would want to upgrade your weapons.
