Lone Wolf


Self Elemination


This operation is for missions that need less work to complete objectives.
This operation is also for avoiding the noise and room of teams to end the
mission in a more non-stressful way. Since there is only one special force solider
in this mission, the best form to be armed is the SWAT, COLT COMMANDO or STRUL AWG
with a SILENTSER. Its highly recommened to use a heart beat sensor or jammer. The
difficultly rises if: there's more wide open spaces, a high number of targets,
or stattered targets.
If you want to upgrade your weapons or want to add more go to www.3dretreat.com
